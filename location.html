Location and Contact

Located at the intersection of the roads that connect Baia Mare, Bistrita and Vatra Dornei, the Spring Pearl chalet is in the shade of the high peaks of the Rodna Mountains. DN15 connects the 3 counties, so it is very easy to reach from any direction. We are at the intersection of the forest roads Caprioara and Ursu.
